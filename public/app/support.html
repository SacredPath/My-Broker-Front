Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?
You can deposit funds by navigating to the Deposits page in your dashboard. We support bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cryptocurrency deposits.
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawal times vary by method: Bank transfers take 3-5 business days, cryptocurrency withdrawals are usually processed within 1-2 hours.
Is my account secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and two-factor authentication to protect your account and funds.
